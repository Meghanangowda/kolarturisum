Famous Places

Antara Gange

Antara Gange is a popular hill destination in Kolar known for its natural caves 
and trekking trails. It is located at an altitude of around 1,226 meters above sea level.

The place is famous for the temple of Lord Shiva and the perennial spring water 
inside the cave. It is also called "Dakshina Kashi" due to its religious importance.

Tourists visit here for adventure activities, nature exploration, and peaceful surroundings.

Kolaramma Temple

Kolaramma Temple is one of the oldest temples in Karnataka, built during the Chola dynasty.
It is dedicated to Goddess Kolaramma, a form of Durga.

The temple showcases beautiful Dravidian architecture with intricate stone carvings 
and sculptures. It has historical and cultural importance in the region.

Visitors come here to experience spirituality, heritage, and peaceful surroundings.

Someshwara Temple

Beautiful carvings and peaceful surroundings. 

Someshwara Temple is a historic temple in Kolar built during the Vijayanagara Empire.
It is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is known for its stunning architecture.

The temple features beautifully carved pillars, detailed sculptures, and a grand gopuram.
It reflects the rich cultural and artistic heritage of South India.

Visitors admire its peaceful atmosphere, architectural beauty, and spiritual significance.
